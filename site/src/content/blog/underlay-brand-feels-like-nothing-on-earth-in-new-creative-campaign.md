Workhouse created **Interfloor's first-ever consumer/retailer brand campaign** for its **Tredaire** carpet underlay range. Three 30" CGI ads featuring the 'Tredaire-nauts' floating through space, strapline **'Feels like nothing on earth'**, set to *Cosmic Dancer* by T.Rex. Launched on Channel 4 during George Clarke's Remarkable Renovations (24 April), across radio, digital and in-store, in two bursts (Apr–Jun, Sep–Nov). Collaborators: Flipbook Studio (animation), Media Performance (media). Quotes from Lynn Bamber (Interfloor Marketing Director) and **Tom Gathercole (Strategy & Growth Lead, Workhouse)**.
