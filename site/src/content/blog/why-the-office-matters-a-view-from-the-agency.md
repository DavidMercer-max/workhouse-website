Creative Industries businesses have always been more flexible on workplace environment, and there's real advantage in embracing the changes of the last couple of years. Remaining agile and adaptable has worked well: plenty of collaborative space, hot desks, quiet rooms and communal areas — designed for both collaborative creative spark and more formal strategic thinking. The challenge has been developing this across office and WFH; investing in the right technology became non-negotiable early on.

Incremental updates to the office interior have created a constant-improvement vibe, and combined with a flexible hybrid week (with a few core rules for collaboration) has enhanced a culture that celebrates individuality and creativity while preserving productivity. The rural location, creative space and agency culture is an increasingly attractive proposition to previously city-dwelling talent. "The happier everyone is at work, the better quality work is produced. Our office environment is pivotal to that — like holding up a mirror, reflecting out our culture and values."
