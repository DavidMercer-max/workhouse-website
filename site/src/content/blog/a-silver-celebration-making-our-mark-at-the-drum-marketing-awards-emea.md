Two nominations, one Silver. Shortlisted **'Brilliant use of advertising'** for the Interfloor Tredaire campaign (alongside Nike, Tiffany & Co, Unilever, Sky Mobile); **won Silver for 'Excellence in design/brand'** for the **rebrand of Forbes Solicitors** (against Renault, Typhoo, Diageo, Breville, M&S). Philosophy line: *"big ideas don't just belong to big brands."*
