Workhouse's Christmas campaign, ideated around the table in the **Ribchester office** with every department represented. **'The 12 Ways of Us'** — 12 individual festive video portraits featuring every team member (shot with Studio Dark in a converted garage studio), run as a 12-day Instagram series. Instead of client gifts, they supported **Re-engage**, the charity reducing loneliness in later life. Theme: creativity and connection live in every person in the agency.
